# Moox Family



Mobile apps and desktop apps for direct Upload of images (also used by Moox Estate etc.), videos and live pics from Mobile or other Services (Google, Apple, other image platforms).

Typical timeline view generated for 

- whole Familiy
- Part of Family
- single kid

Access to other family members, limit access to ...

Themes for boys, girls, families ... (Dino, Robot, ... do lovely and crazy stuff here!)

Components for parents ... Birth ... first word ... first walk .... Tooth, School ... Milestones ... Birthdays of course

Scrapbook-Stickers, Polaroid, Scissors (it's for Mums :-)

Export for print, direct connection to print services worldwide
